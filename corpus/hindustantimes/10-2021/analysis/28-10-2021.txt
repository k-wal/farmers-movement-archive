28-10-2021||News||A Glasgow explainer: Decoding the climate jargon||New Delhi: The Conference of Parties to United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC), called COP, meets annually to review the work under the convention and come with new guidelines to save the world from vagaries of climate crisis. The first COP was held in Berlin in 1995. COP21 in Paris was a key milestone. And from October 31, the world will meet in Glasgow for COP 26.
