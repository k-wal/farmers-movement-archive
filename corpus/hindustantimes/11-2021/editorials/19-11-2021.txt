19-11-2021||Editorials||In defence of India’s climate stance||Few countries have combined a sense of responsibility to the environment and commitment to the future development trajectory of their citizens in the climate debate as India. In Glasgow, India went way beyond what its historic responsibility in contributing to the climate crisis would have entailed — because it could see that the climate crisis is real, its consequences are devastating, the future economic paradigm had to be based on a low-emission trajectory, and every country had to do more than its share to fight it.
