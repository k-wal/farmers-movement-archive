26-01-2022||Today's||Budget will need to tackle Covid hurdles||Global economies have been hit hard by the Covid-19 pandemic, and India is no exception. Post-pandemic economic recovery is a big challenge for all of us. Governments and central banks across the world resorted to fiscal and monetary measures to ward off the negative impact of the crisis. These measures included liquidity infusion, credit enhancement, deficit financing, direct benefit transfers, even the printing of currency and distribution of helicopter money.
