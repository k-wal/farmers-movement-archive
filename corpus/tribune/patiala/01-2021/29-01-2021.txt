29-01-2021|| Patiala||Flute artiste gets Bismillah Khan Award||Flute player Ustad Dr Mujtaba Hussain has been awarded the prestigious Bismillah Khan award by the Government of Bihar for year 2019-20.Dr Hussain is associated with the Department of Dance, Punjabi University. He has already been awarded ‘Bansuri Ratna’ by the university. His father Ustad Peer Bakhsh was a ‘shehnai’ maestro of Bihar while his uncle Ustad Fahimullah Khan was a renowned flute player. Dr Hussain learnt the fine art from these stalwarts.He said: “The award is very dear to me as I hail from Bihar. It is gratifying that the Bihar Government has acknowledged my contribution to music. This award will motivate me to do better as a musician and as a teacher.” — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/patiala/flute-artiste-gets-bismillah-khan-award-204770
29-01-2021||---||Two killed in car-truck collision||Tribune News ServicePatiala, January 28Two persons were killed after their car collided with a truck near Sidhuwal village here today. Family members of the deceased said the car went out of control as another vehicle in its front took an abrupt turn.In his complaint, Balwinder Singh of Rauni village of Bhadson said his relative Atinderjit Singh was commuting along with his two friends Samandeep Singh and Samarpreet Singh whena Bolero car took an abrupt turn in front of their vehicle. As a result, their car went out of control and rammed into a truck coming from the other side, he added.Atinderjit died on the spot while the other two were admitted to a hospital in the city, he said. Later, Samarpreet died during treatment, he added.The police have registered a case against Bolero car driver Karanvir Singh of Chhoti Rauni village of Bashiwala and truck driver Naresh Kumar of Amritsar under Sections 283 (danger or obstruction in public way or line of navigation), 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way), 304-A (causing death by negligence), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others), 338 (causing grievous hurt by act endangering life) and 427 (mischief causing damage to the amount of Rs50) of the IPC.||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/patiala/two-killed-in-car-truck-collision-204772
29-01-2021||Patiala||Two arrested with 39,000 lomotil pills||The police have nabbed two persons and recovered 39,000 pills of lomotil drug from them. The suspects have been identified as Satnam Singh, alias Satta (32), of Rasoolpur Jor village and Gulzar Singh, alias Gari (38), of Bhatedi Kalan village.The Patiala SSP, Vikramjit Duggal, said a police party had laid a naka near Daulatpur village on Wednesday. The police personnel stopped a three-wheeler for checking at the naka, he added. On search, two men travelling in three-wheeler were found carrying 39,000 pills of lomotil drug, he said.Earlier, Satnam used to work as bus conductor, he added. Two cases were already registered against him at different police stations for different crimes, he said. The police arrested the suspects and registered a case under the NDPS Act.||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/patiala/two-arrested-with-39-000-lomotil-pills-204773
