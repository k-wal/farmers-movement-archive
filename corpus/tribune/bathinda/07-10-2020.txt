07-10-2020||Bathinda||HIV blood transfusion: Panel indicts 3 blood bank officials||A three-member inquiry committee of the Health Department has submitted its report indicting three blood bank officials in connection with the transfusion of HIV-positive blood to a minor at the Civil Hospital.The three have been transferred out and the report has been sent to the Health Secretary for departmental action.In the report, the panel said an HIV-positive donor donated blood twice at the hospital’s blood bank. After the incident came to light, various NGOs and social workers had demanded strict action against the erring officials. — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/bathinda/hiv-blood-transfusion-panel-indicts-3-blood-bank-officials-152024
