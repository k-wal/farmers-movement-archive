21-05-2021||Bathinda||Staff back from Patiala, AIIMS forms probe panel||The management of the All-India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) here today constituted a three-member committee to probe the incident of nursing staff being sent back to the parent institution by Government Rajindra Hospital, Patiala, after they alleged poor accommodation and food.Wrong to strike work: directorAIIMS Director Dr DK Singh said: “The nursing staff raised the issue of poor food and accommodation provided to them, which can be sorted out within a day or two. But we have learnt they refused to go to work, which is wrong. We have constituted a committee of three doctors to look into this and submit a report.”He said the recruitment of nursing staff had been pending as the building was under construction. “So we don’t require more staff, but owing to the pandemic, we had hired 500 nursing staff and sent them to the government hospitals to provide them manpower during the crisis,” he said.The newly hired nursing staff were given recruitment letters a day before they were sent to Patiala and Mohali for duty.Around 50 male nurses from AIIMS, Bathinda, deployed at the isolation wing of Rajindra hospital, were relieved by the Medical Superintendent of the hospital on Wednesday after they staged a protest over poor boarding and lodging facilities. They stayed at the Covid ward for only two days.||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/bathinda/staff-back-from-patiala-aiims-forms-probe-panel-255995
21-05-2021||Bathinda||Granthi seeks release of dera chief, arrested||A priest at a gurdwara in Bir Talab village in Bathinda district “offered prayers” for the release of Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim. In a video gone viral on social media platforms, the granthi can be seen doing an ‘ardas’ for a Dalit chief minister in the state and release of the jailed dera chief.After a complaint was given by advocate Harpal Singh Khara and other lawyers to the Bathinda SSP against the priest for making a “politically-motivated ardas” which could vitiate the atmosphere in the state, the police arrested the priest.SSP Bhupinderjit Singh Virk said, “After getting a complaint, we have arrested the priest and booked him under Section 295A of the IPC. We are investigating the matter and further action will be taken based on our findings. There are certain unscrupulous elements who are trying to disturb peace. Their role is being probed thoroughly.” Meanwhile, a letter issued by Kaushal Kumar, Director, National Commission for Scheduled Castes, has sought an action taken report in the matter. The commission has decided to investigate the matter under powers conferred upon it under Article 338 of the Constitution. — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/bathinda/granthi-seeks-release-of-dera-chief-arrested-255998
