15-12-2021||Bathinda||Bathinda rural Congress working president quits||Newly appointed Bathinda rural District Congress Committee (DCC) working president Avtar Singh Goniana resigned over appointment of Bathinda rural Congress halqa in charge Harvinder Singh Laddi’s relative Kulwinder Singh Naruana as Bathinda rural DCC chief.In a letter to the PCC chief, Avtar said, “...Naruana, who has has not participated in any party activity, has been appointed as president... I am ready to work under any other person, but Naruana.” — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/punjab/bathinda-rural-congress-working-president-quits-350414
