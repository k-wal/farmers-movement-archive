17-08-2021||Bathinda||Bathinda: Senior deputy mayor Ashok Kumar’s son booked||Senior deputy mayor Ashok Kumar’s son Tarun Kumar, posted as an executive officer (EO) in Goniana MC, has been booked for assault in a cross FIR at Kotwali police station.Pawan Kumar claimed Tarun, his brother Pankaj Kumar along with some unidentified accomplices assaulted him in a case of road rage. Also, on a complaint by Tarun, a cross FIR was registered against Pawan under various sections, including Arms Act. Tarun said Pawan pointed a revolver at him and threatened to kill him. — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/bathinda/bathinda-senior-deputy-mayor-ashok-kumars-son-booked-298133
17-08-2021||Mansa||Harsimrat Kaur Badal faces ire of Mansa farmers||Bathinda MP Harsimrat Kaur Badal today faced the ire of farmers as they gehraoed her convoy at Burj Rathi village in Mansa district. The protest was led by BKU (Ekta-Dakonda). Harsimrat had come to the village to express her condolences at two houses. Protesters alleged Akali leaders held poll-related meetings in the houses on the pretext of offering condolence to the affected families. — TNS||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/bathinda/harsimrat-kaur-badal-faces-ire-of-mansa-farmers-298136
