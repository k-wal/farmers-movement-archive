In total 115 persons are lodged in the jails,” he added.The Delhi CM met few representatives of Sanyunkt Kisan Morcha, including Prem Singh Bhangu, Rajinder Singh, Deep Singh Wala, Inderjit Singh and Harpal Singh Mundal on Tuesday, who sought help regarding the missing farmers.“So those missing their family members please have a look at this list.
