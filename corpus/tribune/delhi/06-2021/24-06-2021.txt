24-06-2021||New Delhi||Delhi reports 109 new Covid-19 cases, 8 deaths; positivity rate 0.14 pc||Delhi recorded eight deaths and 109 fresh cases of coronavirus infection on Thursday as the city’s positivity rate dipped to 0.14 per cent, according to data shared by the Health department here.The new fatalities have pushed the death toll due to the disease in the city to 24,948.On Wednesday, Delhi recorded 111 Covid-19 cases and seven fatalities, while the positivity rate stood at 0.15 per cent.On June 14, the city had registered 131 cases and 16 deaths, while on February 22, there were 128 cases of Covid-19, according to official figures.On Monday, Delhi had reported 89 coronavirus cases with a positivity rate of 0.16 per cent, both lowest this year so far, while 11 people died, according to the data. 24-06-2021||---||Delhi tourist killed in Himachal mishap, 3 hurt||Mandi, June 23A Delhi tourist was killed in a car accident, near Hull village in Spiti subdivision of Lahaul-Spiti district yesterday evening, while three others were injured. The deceased has been identified as Mohit Yadav (25), son of Vijender Yadav, a native of Delhi.According to the police, the driver lost control over the car near Hull village in the Spiti region and it fell into a deep gorge. At the time of the accident, four persons were travelling in the car. While Mohit died on the spot, three others sustained grievous injuries. They were taken to the Rampur hospital in Shimla district. “The injured are Mohit Yadav, son of Rajesh Yadav of Delhi; Samir Sharma, son of Devender Kumar Sharma, and Barat John, aka Joni, son of Somveer John, both natives of Haryana. They are undergoing treatment and their condition is stated to be stable,” said a police officer.A misjudgment of the road by the driver is said to be the cause of the accident. The police have registered a case under Sections 279 and 304 A of the IPC, and a probe is under way.||https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/delhi/delhi-tourist-killed-in-himachal-mishap-3-hurt-273232
