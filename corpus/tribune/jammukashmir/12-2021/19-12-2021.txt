19-12-2021||Srinagar||Policeman injured as terrorists open fire in Pulwama||Terrorists shot at a policeman in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir on Sunday leaving him injured, officials said.“At about 7.10 pm, terrorists fired at & injured a JKP Follower (a police designation) namely Mushtaq Ahmad Wagay near his residence at Bundzoo,” a police official said.He said the injured has been shifted to Bone and Joint Hospital here for treatment.Further details were awaited.
