The government is working to suppress the movement, there is resentment among our colleagues in the entire peasant movement."Giving an example, Bajwa said: "A woman has got a notice, and she has works in Delhi. Just because her phone was active in the area on Republic Day, she was sent a notice for it.""Have faith in a democratic country, stop harassing people in support of the movement."Farmer leaders have made it clear that the people to whom notices are being sent should refuse arrest. A legal panel set up by farmers will respond to these notices.Appealing to those who have got the notices, the farmer leaders told them not to attend the inquiry without a lawyer.
